# Phase 6 Plan 03: Premise Gate, Evidence Contracts, And Reviewer Evidence Scoring

<objective>
修复生成文章“核心论点不聚焦”和“宏观主张缺证据”的 P0 质量问题，把论点聚焦、证据绑定和 reviewer 评分从提示建议升级为可执行 gate。
</objective>

<threat_model>
- Threat: prompts introduce hardcoded domain facts. Mitigation: facts must come from Evidence Pool or test fixtures, not embedded prompt claims.
- Threat: evidence requirements leak fabricated citations. Mitigation: Writer must bind to evidence IDs; Reviewer penalizes unbound claims; FactCheck work follows in Plan 04.
- Threat: Premise Gate blocks valid broad reports. Mitigation: thresholds configurable and user-confirmable through OUTLINE_REVIEW/PREMISE_GATE metadata.
</threat_model>
